      Small store plenty of stock - Edit

      Saw this place listed on happycow some years ago and have shopped here a couple of times. Was just here again this week - here's my Maui Alive & Well review update.

      The store sells a little of bit everything, from fresh veggies and fruits to nutritional supplements, packaged snack foods, and water bottles. It's well-lit inside and air-conditioned. Prices are slightly more than other health stores on the island, but when you are in this area it's a good option.
