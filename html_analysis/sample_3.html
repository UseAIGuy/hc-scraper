    Bakery

    A small bakery that makes both traditional as well vegan baked goods. Vegan range varies and might include cinnamon rolls, banana muffin, marzipan cookies, snegle, croissants, and cake. Uses honey and dairy in some items. Formerly known as Naturbageriet. Open Mon-Fri 06:30-18:00, Sat-Sun 07:00-16:00.
